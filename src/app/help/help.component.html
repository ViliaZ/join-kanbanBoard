HOW TO USE JOIN

JOIN is based on the kanban system, which is frequently used in professional settings, eg to organize software development in a team. To get started, create your own boards and tasks or use the data provided. Note, that "ToDo"-board is fixed and cannot be edited or deleted.

Quick Start:

1Create a new Task. Use the form on your Dashboard or the colored Plus-button in the top menu of each site. Fill out all required fields.

2Choose a board, where you would like to save your task. Otherwise, tasks are saved to backlog per default.

3The backlog stores all your tasks that are ot in progress yet. You can filter (search) or sort the backlog tasks by clicking on the sort icon.

4Start working on your task by moving it from backlog to "ToDo" board.

5Use drag and drop to move your task between boards. Hover over a task to see edit option and delete option. You can edit a task by clicking on it. Tasks can also be pinned to the top of a board (see Pin icon on hover).

Create your own boards, delete or edit them to your requirements.

Enjoy!
